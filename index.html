About me

Professional with a lifetime's worth of experience as a student. I have keen interests in Computer Science, Investment, Design and Entrepreneurship. I currently have a degree in Computer Science & Design from the Singapore University of Technology and Design.

Prior to university, I've always aspired to be an Entrepreneur that would dabble in the stocks, real estate and technology as I've always been a keen Gamer and Tech Enthusiast. However, I believe that while many Entrepreneurs excel in many non-technical skills many of them lack crucial technical skills that might ultimately play a vital role in their businesses. One might say that it is easy for a doctor to become a businessman, however it would be harder for a businessman to become a doctor.

Thus, due to my passion for technology and computers, I chose to pursue a Computer Science degree in order to empower myself with a strong foundation through computer science to enrich myself in the pursuit of my goals.
