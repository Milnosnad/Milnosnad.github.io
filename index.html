About

Hi, I’m Danson — the result of mixing tech, capital, and a slightly irrational fear of irrelevance.

I studied Computer Science with a specialization in Financial Technology (and, because one buzzword wasn’t enough, I added a minor in AI). Somewhere along the way, I found myself straddling three worlds — entrepreneurship, technology, and finance. I build things when I can, invest in ideas when I can’t, and spend most of my time trying to understand how both worlds actually move.

These days, I work at the intersection of hedge funds, alternative investments, and technology — running deep-dive due diligence, decoding data rooms, and occasionally designing digital tools that make complex finance feel a little more human. It’s a mix of analysis, storytelling, and creation — the perfect overlap of logic and curiosity that keeps me engaged.

Outside of work, I’m into sports, fashion, the gym, biohacking (call me a discount Bryan Johnson), venture ideas, gaming, consulting rabbit holes, and all things creative or slightly unhinged.

I’m always up for good ideas, good people, or questionable plans that just might work. Feel free to reach out and say hi.
